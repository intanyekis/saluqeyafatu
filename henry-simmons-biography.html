Henry Simmons Bio (Wiki)

Publish date: 2024-06-07

Who is Henry Simmons?

Henry Simmons is an American actor mostly known for his role as NYPD Detective Baldwin Jones on the ABC police drama, ‘NYPD Blue’.

Simmons has also appeared in other numerous popular television series including ‘Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D’ and ‘Bones’ among others.

Henry Simmons: Birth, Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Siblings

Henry was born in Stamford, Connecticut on July 1, 1970.

He belongs to an African-American ethnic background.

Henry was born as one of three children of Aurelia(mother) and Henry Simmons, Sr.(father).

He has one sister named Heather M. Simmons.

Education History

He was successful in earning a basketball scholarship to Franklin Pierce University and has a business degree from there.

Initially setting out to start his own business, his interest in acting increased gradually after the completion of college and it didn’t take him long to join the show business.

Henry Simmons: Professional Life and Career

Henry Simmons initially started working in Stamford at Fortune 500.

He had previously experimented in the theater while attending Franklin Pierce College.

After not being satisfied with his job, he moved to New York City and began to study acting.

His first recurring role came after he was included in the cast of ‘Another World’.

He has been part of numerous movies and television series in his career.

Some of the movies, television series that he has appeared in include ‘Saturday Night Live’, ‘Above the Rim’, ‘The Cosby Mysteries’, ‘New York Undercover’, ‘13 Bourbon St.’, ‘Let It Snow’, ‘On the Q.T.’, ‘A Gentleman’s Game’, ‘Are We There Yet?’, ‘Taxi’, ‘Lackawanna Blues’, ‘NYPD Blue’, ‘Something New’, ‘Madea’s Family Reunion’, ‘Pepper Dennis’, ‘The Insurgents’, ‘South of Pico’, ‘Shark’, ‘World’s Greatest Dad’, ‘The Cleaner’, and others.

His portrayal of Detective Baldwin Jones on the ABC drama, ‘NYPD Blue’ has been his most recognizable work.

He plays the role of Agent Alphonso “Mack” MacKenzie on ABC’s show ‘Marvel’s Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D’.

Some of the awards that he has been awarded include the Grand Jury Award for Best Actor at the American Black Film Festival for his portrayal of Dr. Walter Chambers in the South of Pico.

He has also been nominated for BTVA Special/DVD Voice Acting Award.
