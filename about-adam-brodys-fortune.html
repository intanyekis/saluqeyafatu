Adam Brody’s net worth is around $12 million


Adam Brody in Brief

Adam Jared Brody, known for his role as Seth Cohen in “The O.C.”, was born on 15 December 1979, in San Diego, California, USA. He has garnered significant wealth through his successful career in acting, with notable roles in “Mr & Mrs. Smith”, “Jennifer’s Body”, and “Scream 4”.

As of early-2016, his net worth is estimated at $12 million, earned through various television shows, movies, and his work as a writer and musician. Brody is also known for his music projects, including his band “Big Japan”.

Adam Brody’s Background

Adam attended Wangenheim Middle School, then Scripps Ranch High School. Disinterested in academics, he spent most of his time surfing and later dropped out of community college to pursue an acting career in Hollywood. After a year of training, he landed his first role in a television movie called “Growing Up Brady” and subsequently secured minor roles in “American Pie 2”.

He gained recognition for his role in “Gilmore Girls” as Dave Rygalski, but it was his portrayal of Seth Cohen in “The O.C.” that brought him widespread acclaim. Following the series’ conclusion, Brody shifted his focus to films and appeared in productions such as “The Ten”, “Jennifer’s Body” and “The Oranges”.

Aside from acting, Adam is heavily involved in music and writing. He has been featured in various magazines and has received multiple Teen Choice Awards, cementing his status as a widely recognized figure in the entertainment industry. Brody married actress Leighton Meester in 2014 and they have a daughter together.
