Blake Livelys White Airport Outfit: Shop the Look for Less

Publish date: 2024-07-06

All white, all white, all white! Blake Lively has killed her Cannes Film Festival style ever since touching down in France. She arrived at Nice airport on May 9, conquering the baggage claim in a monochromatic look.

The Shallows actress, 28, kept warm in a lightweight Burberry trench with black leather trim. The coat (similar styles start at $850) matched her Burberry pants, which featured a tuxedo stripe. Lively, who’s expecting her second child with Ryan Reynolds, stood tall in brown and gold sandals and toted her carry-ons in a quilted black bag.

Want to be queen of the terminals, too? Us Weekly shopped Lively’s entire look — and it costs less than half the amount of her trench!

2. Ann Taylor Kate Strech Cotton Cropped Pants ($59.50, anntaylor.com)

3. MZ Wallace Small Metro Tote ($195, mzwallace.com)
