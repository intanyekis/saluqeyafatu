Arin Hanson

Publish date: 2024-08-09
On 6-1-1987 Arin Hanson (nickname: Egoraptor) was born in West Palm Beach, Florida. He made his 1 million dollar fortune with Starbomb, Game Grumps & Egoraptor. The celebrity, actor & comedian is married to Suzanne Berhow, his starsign is Capricorn and he is now 37 years of age.
Arin Hanson is best known for his internet animations and he is also known as the founder of YouTube Let's Play web series Game Grumps. He has created multiple popular animated web series on his YouTube channel where he parodied popular cartoons such as Pokemon, Ninja Gaiden etc.

In 2002, he met his wife-to-be, Suzanne Berhow, at a convention. She also has a popular gaming channel titled KittyKatGaming. The two got married in 2013.
