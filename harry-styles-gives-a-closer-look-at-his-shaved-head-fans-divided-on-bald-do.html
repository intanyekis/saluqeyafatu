Harry Styles gives a closer look at his shaved head, fans divided on bald 'do

Publish date: 2024-09-03

Harry Styles’ new look isn’t very pleasing to his fans.

On Wednesday, the “Golden” singer, 29, offered a closer look at his new hairstyle, with his Pleasing beauty brand posting a photo of the freshly shorn look on Instagram.

“Our Founder, Harry Styles, toasts the launch of Pleasing Fragrance with friends in London. November, 2023,” reads the caption; comments were immediately turned off.

That didn’t stop fans from losing their minds over the confirmation — and close-up — of the British star’s bald makeover.

“I’m literally shaking, please delete this,” one person tweeted. “Homie looks like he belongs in a fast and furious movie,” another wrote.

A third demanded he “Unshave it,” while a fourth even joked that Styles’ Pleasing sweater was “made out of the hair he shaved off.”

Other people supported the fresh start, with one writing, “Yesterday he was looking like a kid and today he’s uncle wow.”

“He looks totally different but cool,” another tweeted diplomatically.

The star first sparked outrage when fans caught a quick glimpse of his shaved head at a U2 concert he attended with girlfriend Taylor Russell last week.

While fans are apoplectic over the news, Styles’ shave could simply be a temporary shake-up before the his 30th birthday in February.

The former One Direction member has tested out many lengths over the years, from shoulder-grazing curls to short and floppy locks.

Taylor Swift fans even suspect she referenced Styles’ ever-changing mane in her “1989 (Taylor’s Version)” vault track “Now That We Don’t Talk,” which includes lines like, “You grew your hair long” and, “From the outside it looks like you’re trying lives on.”

Perhaps he’s simply preparing to enter a new year with a new vibe.
