Mujhe Pyar Hua Tha Drama Story

Mujhe Pyaar Hua Tha is the story of the love triangle between Maheer, Saad, and Areeb. Hania Aamir has performed the role of Maheer and Zaviyar Noman Ijaz is performing the role of Areeb. They meet each other and fall in love. Wahaj Ali played the role of Saad, Maheer’s cousin in the drama. He loves Maheer and couldn’t express his love for her.

Areeb wants to marry Maheer but his family disagrees. Her mother does not want Maheer as her daughter-in-law. The love triangle between Maheer, Saad, and Areeb takes them to an unexpected twist in their life.

The drama story is full of romance, entertainment, suspense, and emotions. Viewers will see a new emotional love story with amazing acting. After the super hit drama “Kaisi Teri Khudgarzi” and “Mere Humsafar”, this serial will be an addition to the success story of the Pakistan drama industry.
