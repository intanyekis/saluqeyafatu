Geffri Hightower - Net Worth, Husband &amp; Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-19

Geffri Hightower is an American actress who is popularly known for her role as Simone Hicks in All American and its spinoff All American: Homecoming. Some of her other popular works include Private Practice, WTF, Baron Davis, Snowfall, and Loco.

Hightower's net worth has been estimated to be $5 Million. Her zodiac sign is Pisces. She stands at the height of 5 Feet and 6 Inches (167 cm) and weighs 132 lbs (60 kg). Let's learn a bit more about the Californian actress.

Geffri Hightower has no partner in her life at the moment and is currently enjoying her single life. For now, she is giving all of her time to her career, family, and friends.

Hightower also doesn't speak much when it comes to her love life. She is private about it which explains the fact that her previous relationship records are nonexistent for now. 

What Is Hightower's Education Background?

Hightower went to the University and unlike a lot of movie actors, she didn't drop out and got herself a degree. She has a Bachelor's degree in Mass Communication/Media Studies. 

All American actress, Hightower got her Bachelor's degree from Clark Atlanta University, the same University Darnell Nicole got her Bachelor of Science degree. She was at the University from 2011 to 2015. 

Before Clark Atlanta, Hightower did her basic schooling at Alexander Hamilton High School Academy of Music. However, studying at Atlanta University wasn't Hightower's first choice. She initially decided to pursue her degree at Fordham University, which is a private institute in the Bronx, New York. 

Hightower had even received an offer from the University itself to study but had a change of mind at the last moment and instead went to Clark Atlanta.

Raised By A Single Mother

Geffri Hightower was not born with a silver spoon in her mouth. She didn't have it easy as her father was missing from her life most of the time. Her, mother Stephanie Renee Hightower is the most important woman in her life as she has always been there for her. 

Stephanie has been the biggest inspiration for the All American actress, Hightower. She has revealed that her mother has been her backbone always inspiring her and motivating her to achieve what she wanted from her life.

HuffPost also mentioned in their article that Hightower's mother was very protective of her. The article says that actress wanted to leave her home in California to study but her mother didn't support her decision at the beginning.

Started As A Broadway Actor

Hightower wanted to become an actor ever since she was a kid. Her love for acting is what led her to do broadway shows before movies. She was only nine years old when she made her first appearance on broadway. 

The first-ever role of the Private Practice actress Hightower was playing Young Nala in the popular show, The Lion King. To be a broadway star, you need to learn how to perform on stage. She developed her dancing skills at the Debbie Allen Dance Academy. 

Hightower Take On Black People's Stories In Movies

Hightower, being an African-American woman herself, wants more and more stories from her community to be featured in movies. In an interview with Yahoo!, she said that her people should share their stories with the world using this medium and it shouldn't just be the stereotypical one.

Loco actress, Hightower talked about how at one point black people only wanted to be shown in a certain light in movies. She wants her people to share all of the amazing stories of her community. She added not to limit themselves to certain characters that have historically been shown in movies.

A Quick Summary Of Hightower's Acting Career

Geffri has been actively doing movies since 2006. Most of her filmography is made up of TV works with some of the popular shows of her career so far being Private Practice, WTF, Baron Davis, Snowfall, and All American. 

Hightower's first work as per her IMDb page was the TV show, Jericho, where she played the role of Allison. She was only for one episode of the show. 

It wasn't until 2007, the first big break for Hightower happened. She landed the role of Maya Bennett in Private Practice as a child actor. She was also in Everybody Hates Chris in 2008 for only an episode as Latrinda.

Some of Hightower's other recognizable characters include Kevin-Rose Hankins, Zoe, Lydia, and Khadijah Brown. But, easily her most recognizable work so far in her career has to be the role of Simone Hicks. She played this character in All American and also in its spinoff, All American: Homecoming.
