What is HangEase and Who is the Founder of HangEase?

HangEase is a company that specializes in producing durable, collapsible closet and space management systems. The HangEase product is designed to keep clothing organized, wrinkle-free, and maximize closet space. But who is the mastermind behind this innovative solution? Meet Ryan Landis, the founder and inventor of HangEase.

Ryan Landis, hailing from Plano, Texas, had a brilliant idea at a young age. When he was just ten years old and in the third grade, he envisioned a foldable hanger made of reinforced plastic. This ingenious invention could effortlessly support heavy winter jackets while occupying minimal space in the closet.

The young entrepreneur’s passion for finding a solution to closet clutter and wrinkled clothes led to the birth of HangEase. Ryan’s innovative mindset and determination to create a practical and efficient closet system propelled him to turn his vision into a reality.

With the HangEase product, organizing your clothes and optimizing your closet space has never been easier. Say goodbye to messy wardrobes and hello to a tidy and efficient storage solution!

“HangEase is the result of my childhood curiosity and desire to simplify everyday tasks. I wanted to create a hanger that could accommodate bulky outerwear without sacrificing closet space. The overwhelming positive response to HangEase has been incredibly rewarding.” – Ryan Landis

HangEase Shark Tank pitch

In season 5, episode 27 of Shark Tank, Ryan Landis appeared seeking an $80,000 investment for a 30% stake in his company, HangEase. During the pitch, Ryan demonstrated how the hangers worked and claimed to have previously sold 400,000 hangers at Walmart, resulting in $200,000 in sales and $70,000 in profits. He received a deal from Mark Cuban and Lori Greiner, but the deal did not come to fruition, and HangEase is no longer in business.
