Shaun Gayle Biography-Wiki

Shaun Gayle is a former American football defensive back in the NFL who was born on March 8, 1962, in Newport News, Virginia, the United States as Shaun Lanard Gayle. Gayle played twelve seasons, eleven for the Chicago Bears (1984–1994), and one for the San Diego Chargers (1995).

Gayle was a member of the Bears squad that won Super Bowl XX in the year 1985. Gayle was also a member of the “Shuffling Crew” in the video The Super Bowl Shuffle. He attended Ohio State University.

He owns the distinction of returning the shortest punt for a touchdown in NFL history when Gayle returned a punt five yards for a touchdown against the New York Giants in the Bears 1985 divisional playoff victory. Gayle is now appearing at the Chicago Bears Fan Convention. Gayle currently works as an NFL analyst for Sky Sports, appearing on the weekly NFL broadcast.
