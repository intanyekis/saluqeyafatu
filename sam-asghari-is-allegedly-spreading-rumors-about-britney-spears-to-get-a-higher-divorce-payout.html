Sam Asghari Is Allegedly Spreading Rumors About Britney Spears To Get A Higher Divorce Payout

Publish date: 2024-07-21

Since filing for divorce on August 16, a lot of accusations have come to light about Sam Asghari and Britney Spears’ relationship, including that rumors that she cheated with a staff member, prompting him to file to end their marriage.

However, according to a new report, the cheating accusations are false. Even more, they’re an attempt on Sam’s part to defame Britney in hopes of securing a higher divorce settlement.

Speaking to In Touch, one source disclosed that Spears had engaged in infidelity, which they cited as a reason for their divorce.

However, a second source denied these accusations to the outlet. suggesting that Asghari may have initiated false rumors about Spears' unfaithfulness in an effort to secure a more substantial divorce settlement.

“Britney says the accusations are garbage,” the insider explained. “And it wouldn’t surprise her if Sam came up with the rumor himself, then leaked it through one of his media buddies so he’d have grounds to negotiate a larger settlement or get more out of the prenup.”

It was previously reported by TMZ that insiders allege that Sam holds the belief that Britney requested a staff member to record a video of her in a state of undress. Furthermore, it’s claimed that she had a romantic involvement with at least one male employee.

One informant even told the publication that there exists a video showing Britney and the staff member in a situation deemed "compromising."

The fresh accusations against Britney surfaced following her recent attention-grabbing event - a "divorce party." During this gathering, she posted multiple social media updates where she was seen celebrating with bare-chested men. One video displayed a mystery man playfully licking her leg, while another clip captured Britney being lifted by a group of shirtless men by the poolside.

Sam has denied allegations that he’s planning to challenge the prenup, but reports say the Iranian-born actor was only entitled to $1 million after two years of marriage, whereas he filed for divorce after 14 months. The prenup also waived his rights to Britney’s music catalog and he’s currently not on the deed of the house.

Even if Sam is hoping the rumors about Britney’s infidelity work in his favor, he’s already getting financial support from Britney – the pop star recently agreed to pay for his $10,000 apartment. Their divorce remains ongoing.
