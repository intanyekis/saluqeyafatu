Kevin Jonas News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-08-27

Name:Kevin Jonas

Hometown:Teaneck, NJ

Birthday:November 5, 1987

About

The oldest of the Jonas Brothers trio, Paul Kevin Jonas II, affectionately known as Kevin, was born on November 5, 1987 in Teaneck, NJ.

As part of the band Jonas Brothers, with younger brothers Joe and Nick, the group become multi-platinum sensations. They 2008 the trio starred in the Disney Channel Original Movie, Camp Rock, opposite Demi Lovato. The brothers currently star in a television show on the Disney Channel, JONAS.

Despite their infamous purity rings, Kevin’s brothers have been linked to many stars throughout the years. Kevin, on the other hand, has had a very quiet personal life.

In 2007, he met girlfriend, Danielle Deleasa on vacation in The Bahamas. The couple announced their engagement in July 2009 and were promptly married on December 19, 2009 at Oheka Castle on Long Island, New York. They welcomed their first child, a daughter named Alena Rose, in 2014. The twosome welcomed their second daughter, Valentina, in October 2016.
