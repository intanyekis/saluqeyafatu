Josie Gibson is an English television personality and presenter who emerged as the winner of the 11th series of Big Brother in 2010. In 2019, she started appearing as a regular segment presenter and announcer for This Morning.     Personal Trainer

    After her weight had soared well beyond the acceptable limits, Josie decided to turn it around and thanks to her disciplined fitness regime, she managed to shed a lot of weight. As she disliked long workouts, her personal trainer devised short circuit training workouts that lasted about 21 minutes.

    The elements of cardio and resistance training were combined to help her torch fat more effectively. Overall, she performs such workouts at least twice a week. She has also added yoga to her workout routine.

    She also made changes to her diet and decided to watch what she eats. Overall, she cut back on her sugar consumption. She has also given up eating junk food and chocolate. She allows 20 percent of her diet to be made up of her favorite foods. The other 80 percent is comprised of veggies, salads, lean meats, pulses, and fruits.

    Josie Gibson Facts

  • When she was 14 years old, she left her home and moved to live with her aunt on a farm.
  • Before she participated in the reality show Big Brother, she used to work as a sales representative.
  • In Big Brother, she was the first contestant to enter the house and managed to be the last contestant standing by the time, the show came to an end. She managed to win 77.5% of public votes in the final round of voting, which was incidentally the highest percentage tallied by any contestant in the history of the show.
  • After her stint in Big Brother, she managed to become a certified personal trainer, which has helped her release a fitness book and a couple of fitness DVDs.
  • Josie has done activism and charity work for CLIC Sargent, which is a UK-based children’s cancer charity. She donated £20,000 of her winnings from Big Brother to the charity.
  • Josie appeared on TV shows – Celebrity Impossible and Silent Library to raise funds for her selected charities.
  • In the early months of 2011, she released her exclusive fragrance under the label of Josie.
  • Josie Gibson also released her exclusive range of merchandise starting with customized tops in 2011.
  • In January 2018, Josie and a female pal were assaulted by a taxi driver while they were looking to return home after a night out. She was left with bruises on her chest and a black eye.
