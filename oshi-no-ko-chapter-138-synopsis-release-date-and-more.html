About Oshi no Ko

“Oshi no Ko” is an enthralling manga series that tells the story of a doctor and a patient who, after their deaths, are reborn as twins to a renowned Japanese pop idol. Serialized in Shueisha’s Weekly Young Jump magazine since April 2020, the series has amassed thirteen volumes as of November 2023.

The manga has garnered widespread popularity, leading to an anime adaptation by Doga Kobo. It first aired in April 2023, with a second season slated for 2024. Boasting over 15 million copies in circulation by November 2023, the series has also been released in North America by Yen Press and is available on Shueisha’s Manga Plus platform.
