Where Is Lev Parnas Today?

Lev Parnas is now in prison after he was sentenced to twenty months. Parnas was punished for fraud and campaign finance crimes.

As already mentioned, he is an associate of Rudy Giuliani. However, his crime was not directly related to his work as a fixer for Giuliani.

Instead, the case was discussed after Parnas illegally donated Russian money to U.S. elections. Furthermore, he even lied about the source of funds he contributed to political bodies, especially during the 2020 campaign of Donald Trump.

The United States attorney, Damian Williams announced his punishment in Manhattan federal court. The judge also ordered him to pay $2.3 million in compensation.

Before the sentence was announced, he apologized to the victims who lost their money while investing in his business ventures.
