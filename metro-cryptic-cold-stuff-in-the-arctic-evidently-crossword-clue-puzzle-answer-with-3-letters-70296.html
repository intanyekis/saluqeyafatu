What is Metro Cryptic Crossword Puzzle?

The Metro Cryptic Crossword Puzzle is a type of crossword puzzle offered by Metro, a notable British newspaper1. These crosswords are quite complicated and challenging. Cryptic Crosswords are puzzles that have double meanings. They may seem simple on the surface but they have deeper meanings. The first part of the clue may be a straight answer but the later part of the clue could be a double meaning or entirely different answer1. How to Play Metro Cryptic Crossword Clue?

When you play the Metro Cryptic crossword online, there is a timer present which you can pause if you want to take a break. If you get stuck and can’t figure out the answer, you can get help. Click on ‘’Get Hint’’ and you will receive a hint in the puzzle1. Clicking on ‘’Get Word Hint’’ will reveal the answer. You can also check your answers if they are correct by clicking on ‘’Check Answers’’. False answers are highlighted in red in the puzzle1. If you click on ‘’Show Solution’’, the entire solved puzzle is shown. However, the game will be over. Once you solve a puzzle successfully, you can enter your name and check your ranking on the leaderboard1. If you want to save the game for future use, you can do so as well. Each weekday, a new free cryptic puzzle is added at midnight on Metro. There are also other games like ‘’Codeword’’ and ‘’Word Wheel’’
