Early Life & Background

Udeze was born on August 15.

She was born and raised in Lagos State where she had her primary and secondary education.

She originally hails from Orlu, Imo State.

She obtained a degree in English education at Imo State University.

Career

Udeze started acting in 2013.

She has starred in numerous movies such as Before Getting Married, A week to my wedding, Melting point, Bleeding King and so on.
