The Journey of Mage Horse

Mage Horse’s journey began when it was acquired at an auction of 2-year-olds at the Timonium Fairgrounds. The acquisition of Mage led to a financial scramble, prompting the involvement of Commonwealth Thoroughbreds, an innovative fractional investment platform.
